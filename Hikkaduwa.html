Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

Join us on a memorable tour to catch a glimpse of these majestic yet adorable creatures.

Located right in the heart of Hikkaduwa Town, our facility is a fully fledged turtle hatchery & rescue centre with an experienced staff dedicated to deliver to the delicate needs of these vulnerable creatures. Turtle eggs are safely buried in a temperature controlled hatchery, away from all dangers. Once these eggs hatch, the hatchlings will be moved into our in-house tanks and treated until they are essentially strong enough to combat the dangers of the open ocean. Being a rescue centre, our facility holds many turtles who were injured and rescued. They are given professional veterinary care until they are well enough to dive back into the ocean.

Our visitors will be given the opportunity to catch a glimpse (and even get hold) of the turtles and will be given a guided, well-detailed tour of all the processes related to the turtles.
